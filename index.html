Testing

Testing of this app would be a continuous task that is allocated throughout the project. We want to ensure that the launch of the project is successful and doesn’t have any issues that could have been prevented through the development of the app.
There would be 2 core testing teams, developers and controlled-users.
Developers would ensure there are no issues with the functionality and content of the app. The testing plan will include scripts to be followed and suggestions of different potential user input. The key focus would be testing if all links/buttons are linked to the right pages, the content provided provides the correct content and the back-end coding causes no issues and is consistent throughout. The content would be reviewed and ensured that it is up to date.
Controlled-User testing would be focused on the app’s usability and content to determine whether the app is effective in its goal. We would want to have at least 10 people using the app daily in their normal day to day life with feedback sessions starting with once a week and then moving towards fortnightly feedback sessions. This is to continue improvements to the app and address any issues that may arise. We would also want to bring in new users to test out the app throughout the development stage to ensure that the first experience of using the app is engaging, and encourages the continued use of the app.
The more testing that is provided throughout will increase the change of success of the app. The constant feedback would allow our team to plan and design new components that can be introduced to build the best possible app. If there are still issues that impact users negatively or the app’s content is not useful to our users then the app would be a failure. We want to ensure that the app’s goal is reached and the user experience is smooth.
